Welcome to the Sunday Tennis Club

About us

Formed in 2024, we are the Sunday Tennis Club. The Sunday Tennis Club formed in order to allow casual Tennis players connect and play a friendly hour of Tennis every week on a Sunday, with courts available for booking between 13:00 and 15:00, on the hour.
Members register as a singles or doubles attendee and will be allocated a court according to their preference. We also accept entire court bookings if you're looking to play with a friend you already know, providing you are both already STC members.
For more information about our memberships, please follow the link here.

Facilities

The Sunday Tennis club meets at BlackBrook Leisure Centre on Sunday's between 13:00 and 15:00. We have 5 indoor courts and 6 outdoor courts available for use, with rackets and balls available for use with a safety deposit of £10 (deposit returned in full when equipment is returned).
